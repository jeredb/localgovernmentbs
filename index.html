ABOUT MODULAR LAYOUTS

Modular layouts are mobile sections of a layout that are built to specific standards. The standards ensure that all of the members' modules and any club-owned modules will connect interchangeably. Standards are supplied to all new members and serve as a guide for bench work, wiring, laying track, and building scenery.

Eastern Jackson County Mainliners use Digital Command Control (DCC). DCC allows independent control of locomotive consists and simultaneous operation of multiple trains on a single track. Multi-directional control further enhances prototypical operation as trains meet and pass.

Work sessions are scheduled to repair and upgrade modules, or to build new modules. Members are always available to offer their help and experience to anyone needing assistance in meeting the standards.

The club is always looking for ways to improve as new technology presents itself. We have projects in process and more planned as we keep updating.
